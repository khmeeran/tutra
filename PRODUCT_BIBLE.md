# 1. Product Bible

This is the single source of truth for the Tutra platform's engineering, architecture, and tracking. Every feature is judged not just by its implementation status, but by the emotion it evokes and its success metric.

**Legend:**
* ✅ **Done**: Fully implemented and tested.
* 🟡 **In Progress**: Currently being built or partially working.
* ❌ **Planned**: Not started but architected.
* ⏸️ **Backlog**: Future consideration (V2+).
